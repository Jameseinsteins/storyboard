Who I Am

A simple life that taught me
everything that matters.

I'm James Albert Escuro — I grew up in a simple home. My father left us for another woman, and my mother, without missing a beat, became both parents wrapped in one. She worked, she sacrificed, she provided. She showed me what love looks like when it doesn't give up.

I grew up curious — grabbing a basketball, learning a chord on an instrument, staying up late over a math problem, or cooking something new just to see if I could. I love exploring the unknown, even if I'm still learning to be okay with making mistakes along the way. That curiosity is what drives me forward.
